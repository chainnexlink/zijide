## 附录 · 英文描述（粘到 B2 描述）
```
WarRescue is a civilian early-warning and safety companion. It aggregates risk information from public sources (air raids, shelling, disasters) and pushes alerts based on your location, with shelter navigation, offline maps and one-tap SOS — helping you and your family decide and take cover faster.

Key features
• Real-time alerts: location-based warnings of nearby air raids / shelling / disasters to buy you precious time.
• Shelter navigation: nearby shelters and safe points on the map, with evacuation routes.
• Offline maps: pre-download your area to view shelters and routes even without internet.
• One-tap SOS: in an emergency, send a located distress message to the emergency contacts you set (SMS / call / push).
• Family mutual-aid: create a family group, share safety status and confirm each other are safe.
• Simulation drill: a built-in drill mode to get familiar with the alert-and-shelter flow anytime.

Important notes
• This app is a civilian safety reference tool. Information is aggregated from public sources, is not an official warning channel, and accuracy, completeness or timeliness cannot be guaranteed.
• In an emergency, always follow official warnings and local emergency authorities. This app is for supplementary reference only.
• One-tap SOS is sent to the emergency contacts you set yourself; the app does not dial any official emergency numbers.

Subscription
• Two auto-renewable subscriptions (monthly): Personal and Family. Family lets you enable multiple family members and share safety status.
• New subscribers get 50% off the first month.
• Refer friends with your code — when a friend signs up you earn a 50%-off renewal coupon (valid 3 months, one coupon per renewal).
• Prices and duration are shown clearly on the purchase page. Subscriptions auto-renew and can be canceled anytime in Settings › Apple ID › Subscriptions. Purchase means you agree to the Terms of Use and Privacy Policy.

For any questions or feedback, contact us via the support page.
```
