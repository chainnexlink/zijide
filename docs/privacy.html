WarRescue Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-30

We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains how the “WarRescue” app (the “App”) collects, uses and protects your information.

1. Information we collect

  1. Location (precise and coarse): to push alerts for your area, show nearby shelters and plan evacuation routes.
  2. Contact information (name, email, phone, and the emergency contacts you set): for your account, family mutual-aid and one-tap SOS.
  3. Emergency medical info (e.g. blood type, allergies, medical history — provided voluntarily): to share necessary information with your emergency contacts during a distress call.
  4. Purchase information: to process and restore in-app subscriptions.
  5. Device identifiers and push token: to deliver alert notifications.
  6. User content (mutual-aid chat, support records): to provide mutual-aid and customer-support features.

2. How we use information

The information above is used only to provide the App's warning, safety, SOS, mutual-aid and subscription features. We do not use it for advertising or cross-app / website tracking, and we do not sell your personal information.

3. Information sharing

  • A one-tap SOS distress message is sent only to the emergency contacts you set yourself.
  • To deliver push, SMS / voice and similar features, we transmit the necessary data to service providers (e.g. push and communication providers) solely to complete those features.
  • We may disclose information when required by law.

4. Data storage and security

Data is transmitted over standard HTTPS encryption and stored on access-controlled servers. We apply reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information.

5. Your rights

You can view, edit or delete your profile and emergency contacts in the App; delete your account to remove its data; and manage location and notification permissions in your system settings.

6. Children

The App is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect their personal information.

7. Disclaimer

The App is a civilian safety reference tool. Information is aggregated from public sources, is not an official warning channel, and its timeliness, completeness or accuracy cannot be guaranteed. In an emergency, follow official local guidance.

8. Changes

We may update this policy and will post the latest version on this page.
