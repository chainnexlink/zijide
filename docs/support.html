FAQ

  • Not receiving alerts? Allow Notifications and Location for the app in your system settings.
  • Want to try the full flow? Open Settings → Simulation Drill in the app.
  • How does one-tap SOS work? First set your emergency contacts in the app; in an emergency, one tap sends them a located distress message.
  • Subscriptions & Restore Purchases? On the subscription page you can view prices, manage your subscription and Restore Purchases; you can also manage it in iPhone Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions.
