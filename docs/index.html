WarRescue

Civilian early-warning & safety companion

WarRescue aggregates risk information from public sources (air raids, shelling, disasters) and pushes alerts based on your location, with shelter navigation, offline maps and one-tap SOS to help you and your family decide and take cover faster. This app is a safety reference tool and is not an official warning channel.
